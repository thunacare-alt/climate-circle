Decarbonization solutions for hard-to-abate sectors

Cement

Products

Engineered fuels and materials for circular decarbonization

From kiln-grade plastic AFR to agri-pellets and plastics-in-bitumen modifiers — designed for industrial reliability, not just lab performance.

Plastic-Derived AFR

High-CV alternate fuel for cement kilns and high-temperature applications, supplied to spec with lab-backed QA. Typical CV 4,000–5,500 Kcal/kg.

Agri-Residue Pellets

Densified biomass pellets for co-firing and steam programs with assured sourcing, densification and delivery.

Steam / Industrial Fuels

Custom fuel blends for boilers and process steam, tuned for stability, emissions and existing burner hardware.

Plastics in Bitumen

Processed waste-plastic modifiers for bitumen mixes, aligned with Indian road standards and deployment learnings.

CBG (with AMI Group)

Compressed bio-gas produced in partnership with AMI Group — clean energy from organic waste streams.

Waste Intelligence Platform

Track feedstocks, flows and outcomes; MRV-ready data for chain of custody and verified credit documentation.
