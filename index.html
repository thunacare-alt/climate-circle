How it works

From waste to verified energy in five steps

Collection & segregation

Plastics and crop residue are sourced and sorted at the point of generation.

Cleaning, shredding & densification

Feedstock is cleaned, size-reduced and densified into a uniform fuel.

Fuel spec & QA

Each batch is lab-tested for calorific value, moisture and chlorine to your spec.

Delivery & utilization

Fuel is delivered to the kiln or boiler with changeover support and SOPs.

Tracking & audit reports

Every tonne is tracked end-to-end with audit-ready CO₂-abatement reporting.
